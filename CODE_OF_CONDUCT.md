## Our Standards

**Examples of good behavior:**
- Being kind and respectful in all interactions
- Giving helpful and constructive feedback
- Helping new contributors and beginners
- Focusing on what is best for the students who use this project

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**
- Rude, insulting, or dismissive comments
- Spamming issues or pull requests
- Sharing others' personal information without permission
- Any form of harassment

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and handled fairly.
